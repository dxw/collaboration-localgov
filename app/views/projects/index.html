What problem are we working on?

How can we reduce the number of missed bin collections?

Why is this a problem?

  • Missed bin collections are a regular problem for our county.
  • We are about to move to alternating household and recycling collections, which will mean some adjustment for users.
  • We also recognised last Christmas we had a lot of calls about when bin collections will be moved to around the public holidays.
  • Missed bins represents a significant, yet avoidable cost for the council.
  • We wanted to avoid the same problem this year.

What outcomes are we looking for?

  • Reduce the number of missed collections.
  • Users are more able to "self-serve" and find information for themselves.
  • Reduce number of calls to the customer service team about bin collection times.
  • Missed bins represents a significant, yet avoidable cost for the council.
  • Create savings by reducing the number of call outs to collect missed bins.

What we have done so far

Reports
